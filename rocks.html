Software I love

Some lovely software that I want to share

  • Neo-Vim
    • Its like the arch of text editors, the bleeding edge of text editors. One of the other reasons I use it aside from it being bleeding edge is that more of the community contributes to it unlike vim.
    • Some plugins I use are
      • Vim-polyglot for better syntax support
      • Auto-pairs for inserting brackets in pairs
      • Coc for auto complete
      • Vim-airline for cool bar thing
      • Vim-css-color for hex color highlight
  • Qutebrowser
    • Very based minimal but feature filled browser. Might switch it out though because its written in python(eww) and kinda slow.
  • mpv
    • Obviously.
  • nsxiv
    • Like sxiv(simple x image viewer) but actually being updated because the sxiv repo became read only.
  • Newsboat
    • Light rss reader.
  • Oksh
    • Linux port of OpenBSD's ksh.
  • Doas
    • Using doas instaed of sudo because I feel that sudo has superseded being a way to execute root commands and doas is just a light and simple way to do that.
  • Mupdf
    • Just werks.
